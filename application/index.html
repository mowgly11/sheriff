REQUIREMENTS

Ensure you meet our basic requirements before applying to join our department.

Age Requirement

Must be at least 21 years of age at the time of application.

Education

High school diploma or GED required. College education preferred.

Physical Fitness

Must pass physical fitness test and maintain good physical condition.

Background Check

Clean criminal record and passing background investigation required.

Driver's License

Valid driver's license with good driving record required.

US Citizenship

Must be a United States citizen or permanent resident with valid documentation.
